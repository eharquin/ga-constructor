# PGA(2,0,1) — Projective Geometric Algebra ℝ(2,0,1)

Plane-based 2D geometry: uniform algebra of points, ideal points (directions),
lines, and Euclidean rigid motions.

## Basis & metric

| 1   | e0  | e1  | e2  | e01 | e02 | e12 | e012 |
|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|------|
| +1  | 0   | +1  | +1  | 0   | 0   | -1  | 0    |

Signature: e0² = 0, e1² = +1, e2² = +1.

## Cayley table

|       | 1     | e0    | e1    | e2    | e01   | e02   | e12   | e012  |
|-------|-------|-------|-------|-------|-------|-------|-------|-------|
| **1**    | 1     | e0    | e1    | e2    | e01   | e02   | e12   | e012  |
| **e0**   | e0    | 0     | e01   | e02   | 0     | 0     | e012  | 0     |
| **e1**   | e1    | -e01  | 1     | e12   | -e0   | -e012 | e2    | -e02  |
| **e2**   | e2    | -e02  | -e12  | 1     | e012  | -e0   | -e1   | e01   |
| **e01**  | e01   | 0     | e0    | e012  | 0     | 0     | e02   | 0     |
| **e02**  | e02   | 0     | -e012 | e0    | 0     | 0     | -e01  | 0     |
| **e12**  | e12   | e012  | -e2   | e1    | -e02  | e01   | -1    | -e0   |
| **e012** | e012  | 0     | -e02  | e01   | 0     | 0     | -e0   | 0     |

## Plane-based geometry

| object        | formula                                          |
|---------------|--------------------------------------------------|
| origin        | `e12`                                            |
| points        | `e12 + x·e01 + y·e02`                            |
| ideal points  | `x·e01 + y·e02`                                  |
| lines         | `a·e1 + b·e2 + c·e0`                             |
| join          | `p1 ∨ p2`  — line through two points             |
| meet          | `l1 ∧ l2`  — intersection of two lines           |
| rotors        | `exp((α/2)·B) = cos(α/2) + sin(α/2)·B`           |
| motors        | `exp((δ/2)·B) = 1 + (δ/2)·B`  — translators      |

## Sub-algebras

| sub-algebra                          | blades                                                  |
|--------------------------------------|---------------------------------------------------------|
| Scalars                              | `1`                                                     |
| Complex numbers                      | `1, e12`                                                |
| Hyperbolic numbers                   | `1, e1`  or  `1, e2`                                    |
| Dual numbers                         | `1, e0`  or  `1, e01`  or  `1, e02`  or  `1, e012`      |
| Even sub-algebra ℝ(2,0,1)⁺           | `1, e01, e02, e12`                                      |

## Notes

- 2D PGA is the smallest algebra to contain (as even sub-algebra) natural
  representations of all Euclidean distance-preserving transformations SE(2),
  as well as natural representations of points, ideal points (free vectors),
  and lines.
